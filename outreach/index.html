About the Game

Year: 2025

Developers: Jonas Flückiger, Max Mendes, Nicolas Berger, Marvin George

Genre: 2D Top-Down, Online Co-op, PvP, Hide & Seek

Setting: A dark laboratory where a goat and its robot companions must escape while evading a vigilant security guard!

Game Overview

In this thrilling escape game, a goat and at least two robot goats (iGoats) find themselves breaking free from their containment after an unexpected power outage. While the security guard patrols with only a flashlight to illuminate the darkness, players must work together strategically:

  • The real goat must escape before time runs out
  • Robot goats need to activate terminals to open the exit
  • Only the real goat can jump through windows
  • Robot goats can be revived by the real goat
  • The guard must rely on their flashlight in the dark lab

Characters

The Goat

The star of the show! Only you can jump through windows and rescue your robot friends. Your mission: escape the lab before time runs out!

iGoat (Robot)

A technological marvel! Work with other robots to activate terminals and create diversions. Help the real goat escape, even if it means sacrificing yourself!

Security Guard

Armed with just a flashlight, you must navigate the dark lab to catch the escapees. Can you tell which goat is real and which are robots?

Game Controls

Keyboard

  • WASD: Movement
  • E: Interact
  • ENTER: Chat
  • TAB: Cycle Spectator
  • SPACE: Exit Spectator
  • ESC: Settings

Quick Guide

  • For Goats: Find and activate three terminals to open the exit. Use windows for quick escapes!
  • For iGoats: Protect the real goat and create distractions. You can be revived if caught!
  • For Guards: Use your flashlight wisely - the real goat can't hide forever!
  • Winning: Goats win if the real goat escapes. Guard wins by catching the real goat or preventing escape!
