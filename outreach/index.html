About the Game

Year: 2025

Developers: Jonas Flückiger, Max Mendes, Nicolas Berger, Marvin George

Genre: 2D Top-Down, Online Co-op, PvP, Hide & Seek

Setting: A dark lab where a goat and its robot friends must escape while a security guard hunts them down!

Concept

Inspired by classic cops-and-robbers, iGOAT is a tense escape game. The goat and at least two robot goats break out of their cage after a power outage. The security guard must catch them before the real goat escapes. Robot goats activate terminals to open the exit. Only the real goat can jump through windows. Robot goats can be revived. The lab is dark— the guard sees only with a flashlight. Time is ticking!

Roles

Goat

Escape within the time limit! Only the goat can jump through windows and rescue robot goats.

iGoat (Robot Goat)

Activate terminals to open the exit or help free the goat if it's caught.

Security Guard

Catch all goats or prevent the real goat from escaping. Sees only with a flashlight in the dark lab.

Controls

Keyboard

  • Move Up: W
  • Move Down: S
  • Move Left: A
  • Move Right: D
  • Interact: E
  • Chat: ENTER
  • Spectator Mode: TAB (cycle), SPACE (exit)
  • Settings: ESCAPE

Controller

  • Move Up: DPad Up
  • Move Down: DPad Down
  • Move Left: DPad Left
  • Move Right: DPad Right
  • Interact: Button A
  • Chat: Button Y
  • Settings: Start

How to Play

  • Goat: Escape and jump through windows. Can rescue iGoats.
  • iGoat: Activate terminals to open the exit or free the goat.
  • Guard: Catch all goats or stop the real goat from escaping.
  • Terminals: Three random terminals must be activated to open the exit.
  • Exits: After activating the terminals, the goat can escape through the exit.
  • iGoat Station: The goat can revive iGoats here.
  • Windows: Only the goat can jump through windows.
